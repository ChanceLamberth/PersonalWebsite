Portfolio Project

I have always had an interest in financial investing, especailly in the stock market. I opened up my first brokerage account when I was 16, and I began for myself since ever since. Throughout the years as I dedicated more time to understanding the financial markets, my knowlege grew. I spent a lot of time trying to understand why the markets when up and down, but I still to this day can never guess correctly. If you find a way, let me know.

Since my quest for finding why markets go up and down hit a dead end, even with my Economics degree. I decided to head down a different path. Over time, particually leading up to college and in my first two year of college my investing knowlege grew tremendously. Through my extracurricualar research I discovered rebalancing and other factors that help portoflio's grow and maintain their returns. I was particually interested in building a portfolio that could withstand all types of market conditions and make money in good times or bad. I started tracking and collecting data on the portoflio through Google Sheets.

As I collected data, I that the brokerages I was using lacked rebalancing tools. I was shocked becuase this is a fundamental tool all investors should have at his or her disposal. After taking getting my first does of a computer science course - not really computer science becuase the class was focued on excell - I built a rebalancing model in my google sheet along with collecting data on my portoflio. This rebalancing model took in a few inputs like price and quanity and spit out the amount of shares I needed to buy or sell to reach my targeted amount, how much money I needed to allocate, and where or not the cost of rebalcing will make me occur a capital gain or capital loss. I was proud of the work in this project and this is still the base of all my portoflio projects.
